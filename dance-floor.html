DANCE FLOOR

Share



With hundreds of square metres of dance floor panels in stock, we can build pretty much any size of dance floor you require – even if if you need an enormous 40ft x 40ft installation. We have a wide variety of dance floor styles for you to choose from, including but not limited to LED dance floors, black and white dance floors, starlit options, and wooden/parquet dance floor options.

With dance floor hire from QOKO Events, you don't just receive the physical flooring. You benefit from delivery, installation from our expert team, and collection back to our warehouse at the end of your event. As specialists in turnkey event production packages for events such as weddings, we usually supply dance floors as part of an overall event equipment hire package that covers all elements of your production. However, we are more than happy to help with your stand-alone dance floor hire requirements.

Hire Dance Floors from Sussex's Leading Supplier

Our dance floor hire options are perfect for a broad spectrum of events. Whether you need one for your office Christmas party or your wedding reception, your choice will, without doubt, depend on several factors such as the available space at your venue, your preferred style of dance floor, and whether you want to go for a starlit LED dance floor to add the wow factor to your occasion.

There are certainly no hard and fast rules when it comes to choosing the right dance floor. However, rest assured that our experienced team can walk you through each option, allowing you to make an informed decision regarding the perfect choice for your specific event.

QOKO Events supply dance floors to both indoor and outdoor events. Our logistics team can liaise with you to ascertain the best access options and look at the best way to set up your chosen dance floor at your venue. Most installations are quick and painless, with several interlocking panels coming together to lay the floor area for your guests to dance on later.

Lastly, all of our dance floor hire options are thoroughly cleaned before and after the event to ensure that all surfaces are pristine, scratch and scuff-free, and ready for a full day or evening of dancing. We also accompany all of our dance floors with gently sloping aluminium edging to prevent trips and falls when stepping onto (and leaving) the dance floor.

LED DANCE FLOOR HIRE FOR SUSSEX WEDDINGS, PARTIES, AND CORPORATE EVENTS

Firstly, they look incredible at weddings, one of our central markets of operation for turnkey event productions. A white dance floor with tiny LED lights giving the appearance of stars looks classy and adds a real sense of style to proceedings. There's something special about that first dance as a couple with the appearance of stars beneath your feet.

Secondly, they are incredibly versatile. Since they are constructed using individual panels, you have flexibility regarding how many panels you would like to use and what shape you would like your LED dance floor to be. You could have sections that depart from the dance floor that lead guests to other significant venue areas, such as the photo booth.

However, LED dance floors don't necessarily have to be white and bejewelled with LED stars. You could, if you wish, hire our retro 70s and 80s style dance floor that uses LED technology to light up squares of the dance floor in different colours. Alternatively, if you feel that white is a colour best reserved for weddings, you can hire a starlit LED dance floor in black for your corporate event to really accentuate those star-like LEDs.

Since our LED dance floor hire services are so dependent on your specifications, it's almost impossible to price online. Rather than order online, it's best to contact our sales team to discuss prices, venues, and how much space within the room you have available.

PLAIN BLACK AND WHITE DANCE FLOOR HIRE FOR SUSSEX EVENTS

Both black and white dance floors have been event favourites for many years. Our teams have probably installed hundreds of black or white dance floor hire options over the years; such is their popularity. From a pragmatic perspective, they give you all of the benefits of the LED dance floors mentioned above whilst coming in at a cheaper price point. Yes, they might not have the bright lights of a top-of-the-range LED dance floor, but we ensure they look every bit as shiny and sparkly.

If your event equipment hire budget is slightly tighter, we recommend choosing one of these two stalwarts. Once again, they afford you the flexibility to create the dance floor you want. Whether you want an elongated rectangle spanning the width of the DJ area, or you would prefer a perfect square, we can create whatever size and shape you need us to.

Once again, these dance floor hire options are perfect for pretty much any event requiring a dance floor. It doesn't matter whether it's one of the most important events of your life (such as a wedding) or an annual company awards dinner that ends with a night of drinking and dancing. Of course, we also make sure they are immaculate before they leave the warehouse and thoroughly clean and polish them after collection.

If you want a robust dance floor that will draw in your guests, don't hesitate to consider a plain black or white option. Or, if you want to mix things up a bit, we can also make a black and white chequered dance floor hire option if you want us to.

WOODEN DANCE FLOOR HIRE FROM SUSSEX-BASED QOKO EVENTS

In our experience, these dance floors are hired for smaller or more intimate events. However, there is absolutely nothing stopping you from hiring over two hundred square feet should you wish! Once again, they are made up of interlocking panels, giving you flexibility in how you would like to set up your dance floor display.

If you do indeed intend to hire a wooden dance floor, we have several size options available such as 12′ x 12′ (144 sqft), which caters to around 60 dancers. If you want to up the ante, you might want to upgrade to a 16′ x 16′ (256 sqft) dance floor, which will comfortably fit over 100 guests.

As with most of our dance floor hire options, we have a few different design types available. If you want to go for a parquet wooden dance floor because it better fits your event theme, then so be it. But we also stock more traditional hardwood dance floors if that is more to your taste or event décor.

As always, the team here at QOKO Events will be on hand to discuss any questions you have regarding wooden dance floors and talk you through our range of wooden dance floor hire options for your event.
